Our Sponsors

The Social Welfare Development Club aims to serve the community by organizing events and activities that promote social welfare and development.

You can join the club by filling out the membership form available on our website or by attending one of our regular meetings.

The club organizes a variety of events including blood donation drives, community clean-up projects, and educational workshops.

Social Welfare and Development Committee with the motto of "Empowering Society, Transforming Lives" has been helping society for several years and continues to do so in any circumstance.
