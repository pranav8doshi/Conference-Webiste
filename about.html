About the Conference

The conference aims to unite individuals dedicated to societal betterment, providing a platform to share impactful ideas for social welfare. It will tackle pressing social issues and propose innovative solutions through dynamic presentations and collaborative round-table discussions.

It will cover vital areas such as education, healthcare, technology, and the environment.

By featuring inspiring speaker sessions, it strives to raise awareness and cultivate responsible, proactive citizens committed to driving positive change in our society.

Social Welfare & Development
Committee

The Social Welfare and Development Committee of VIT Pune has embarked on a remarkable journey of compassion and positive change. With a steadfast commitment for making a difference, our team has tirelessly worked across various domains, reaching out to the vulnerable and fostering a sense of hope and empowerment.

With various flagship events and GP2 Activities, we also place a strong emphasis on social services, ensuring that essential support reaches those in need. Our NSS (National Service Scheme) camps provide opportunities for volunteers to make a tangible difference, while our blood donation drives save lives and encourage a spirit of generosity.

Beyond the numbers, we have witnessed the profound strength of the human spirit, reminding us of the incredible power of kindness and empathy.

Website Link: https://swd.vit.edu/

Vishwakarma Institute Of Technology, Pune

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, part of the Bansilal Ramnath Agrawal Charitable Trust, has been a symbol of academic excellence since its establishment in 1983. The institute proudly holds a NAAC accreditation of Grade 'A++', reflecting its commitment to high educational standards.

The college frequently organizes diverse activities to strengthen students' educational foundations and foster social awareness. The institution encourages students to explore new ideas and consistently supports their initiatives, promoting their holistic development.

In keeping with its tradition of innovation and social responsibility, VIT hosts various conferences and events. These activities emphasize the institute's dedication to both academic and personal growth, reinforcing its reputation as a leading educational institution in Pune.
